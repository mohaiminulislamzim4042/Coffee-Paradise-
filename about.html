Founded by Md. Mohaiminul Islam Zim, Coffee Paradise was born out of a pure passion for the perfect roast. What started as a small dream to serve the best coffee in the neighborhood has now evolved into a community for coffee lovers.

We travel to the most remote corners of the world to find ethically sourced, 100% organic beans. Our master roasters then bring out the unique character of each bean, ensuring that every cup you hold is a masterpiece of flavor and aroma.
